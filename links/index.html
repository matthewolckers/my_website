Writing

  • Varanya Chaubey’s Little Book of Research Writing is fantastic for help with structure.
  • Deirde McCloskey’s Economical Writing points out that most writing is crap at the first draft and shows how to improve the final product.
  • Amanda Agan’s collection of links.

Literature reviews

  • See the literature as a network.

Seminars

  • Jonathan Shewchuk’s advice on giving academic talks. Filled with so many gems such as “The plain absurdity of modern academic talks would be glaring if we hadn’t all pickled in it for so long”.
